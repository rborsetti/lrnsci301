## On-slide copy

# The Game

**GOAL**
Repair every system on the ship and bring the astronauts safely home.

**HOW TO PLAY**
Teacher opens Mission Control on a laptop or projector. Each team joins from one phone with the mission code. Every student has a printed *Flight Engineer's Logbook*. For each broken system the team copies its numbers onto the matching logbook page, folds and cuts a paper strip to find one unit, then types the answer on the phone to restore the system.

**HOW TO WIN**
Restore all 10 systems and splash down on Earth. Choose Competition (live race) or Story (own pace).

## Speaker notes (~85 words / ~34 sec at 150 wpm)

Walk through the loop in one breath. Teacher opens Mission Control on the laptop, students join from one phone per crew with the code, every student gets a logbook. For each system: copy numbers from console to logbook, fold the paper strip to find one unit, type the answer to restore the system. Win condition: all systems restored. Two modes let the teacher dial the energy: live race or low-stakes story.
